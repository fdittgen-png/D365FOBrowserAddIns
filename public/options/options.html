Keyboard shortcuts

  • Alt+Shift+R — start/stop recording
  • Alt+Shift+S — take snapshot
  • Alt+Shift+N — add note
  • Alt+Shift+P — pause/resume

You can remap these in chrome://extensions/shortcuts or edge://extensions/shortcuts.
